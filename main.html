Coffee Tale

The Coffee Tale cafe was founded in 2010 in Astana. The main activity is the preparation of Italian thin dough pizza, as well as the preparation of delicious coffee. Our guests can taste 50 kinds of coffee and more than 40 kinds of pizzas, delicious dishes of Italian, European and Oriental cuisine.
Coffee Tale is the perfect place to meet friends and loved ones. We do our best to make our guests satisfied with the level of service, quality and taste of our dishes. It is important for us that each of our guests leaves us happy and well-fed and comes again and again. After all, a satisfied guest is the result of our joint work.
Your choice is appreciated here. Here you are always welcome.
